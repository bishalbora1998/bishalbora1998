##  Featured Projects

###  [Aviation Safety Incident Retrieval System (RAG)](https://github.com/bishalbora1998/aviation-safety-rag)
Built a full **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** pipeline on 2,634 real NASA ASRS aviation incident reports. Uses `BAAI/bge-large-en-v1.5` for dense semantic embeddings, a cross-encoder reranker for precision, and `FLAN-T5-large` for grounded answer generation. Outperforms keyword search by capturing semantic similarity across varied aviation terminology.  
`Python` `HuggingFace` `PyTorch` `NLP` `Generative AI` `RAG` `sentence-transformers`

###  [Loan Approval A/B Test Analysis](https://github.com/bishalbora1998/loan-approval-ab-test)
Designed and executed a two-stage A/B test in R comparing legacy vs redesigned loan-review models across 38 loan officers. Identified a **42% increase in decision accuracy** (Cohen's d = 2.94) and **18% improvement in bad loan detection** — all statistically significant (p < 0.05).  
`R` `Welch t-Test` `Cohen's d` `A/B Testing` `Statistical Inference` `ggplot2`

---

###  [Cardiovascular Disease (CVD) Rate Analysis](https://github.com/bishalbora1998/cvd-analysis)
Analysed CVD rates across UK regions using correlation analysis and multiple linear regression. Identified overweight as the strongest predictor (+0.319 correlation) and uncovered a counterintuitive negative poverty-CVD relationship — explained through socioeconomic healthcare access disparities.  
`R` `Multiple Regression` `EDA` `Correlation Analysis` `ggplot2` `Public Health`

---

###  [Customer Satisfaction Analysis — Furniture Retail](https://github.com/bishalbora1998/customer-satisfaction-analysis)
Analysed drivers of customer satisfaction using multiple regression and interaction modelling. Key finding: delivery time has a **significantly stronger negative effect in low SES stores** than high SES stores (confirmed via ANOVA and emmeans). Staff satisfaction was the strongest overall predictor (R² = 20.6%).  
`R` `Multiple Regression` `ANOVA` `Interaction Analysis` `emmeans` `Retail Analytics`

---

###  [UK Road Safety Dashboard](https://github.com/bishalbora1998/uk-road-safety-dashboard)
Exploratory data analysis on 100,000+ UK road accident records (STATS19 dataset). Identified peak accident hours, high-risk road types, and the impact of weather and road surface conditions on casualty severity. Visualised findings in an interactive Power BI dashboard.  
`Python` `pandas` `EDA` `Power BI` `Data Cleaning` `Public Sector Analytics`
